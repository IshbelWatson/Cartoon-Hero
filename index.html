Garfield

About Garfield

Garfield is ginger cat who doesn't like Mondays but he loves lasagna and naps.
He's a cynical and lazy feline, who's days are filled with dreaming of delicious food and outsmarting his owner Jon, he also has a mischievous side, often causing trouble for Jon's dog Odie.
Despite his grumpy exterior, Garfield's antics and loveable quirks have made him a favorite comic strip character for generations.


Garfield looking dapper in a top hat

Garfields Characteristics

  1. Lazy: Garfield cherishes his sleep and relaxation time above all else.
  2. Food-Obsessed: Lasagna is his ultimate comfort food, but he'll devour anything in sight.
  3. Sarcastic & Cynical: Garfield has a dry wit and a dim view of the world, often delivering snarky comments.

Friends

Garfield isn't the most social creature but he has a few complex friendships.
Click the links below to find out more.

  • Odie: Jon's goofy dog is Garfield's constant companion. While they often prank each other, they share a weird codependent bond. Odie might be considered Garfield's begrudging best friend.
  • Arlene: A wise and sarcastic neighbor cat, Arlene is Garfield's sounding board and occasional accomplice in schemes. They share a cynical outlook and enjoy witty banter.
  • Penelope Pussycat: This beautiful orange cat lives at an Italian restaurant and is Garfield's love interest. Their relationship is more flirtatious than truly friendly, but Garfield does show a softer side around her.
  • Jon: Garfield's long-suffering owner, is a clumsy cartoonist with a love life that constantly leaves him frustrated.
